Meaning Of “Virgin”

Virgin is the word which is mainly used for 0% adulteration and 100% pure product. Many companies use this word for their purity and natural products.

Basically this word came from Spain just because of Diffentiate a particular product of a company and now spreaded the word for the product's purity, but some time people don't understand the word "virgin" as the company want to portray, they just want to say these products are 100% pure and not using any other product or chemical in it.

So "Virgin" means nothing, it is just to show their product is 100% natural and Pure.

Extra virgin is also similar product like virgin product, for Ex Extra virgin coconut oil is made of very early aged coconuts just of 10-15 days old coconut. it is the purest form of coconut oil made by a Particular type of low pressure expeller and also called "Mother of all Oil".
